Find the ID of you collection

  1. 1) Go to your steam profile (on web browser)
  2. 2) Click on workshop items (right hand coloumn)
  3. 3) Click on collections tab
  4. 4) Go to the page of your collection
  5. 5) Copy and paste the numbers after 'id=' in the URL (only the numbers or it will not work)

Setting the ID in options

  1. 1) Click the Steam Icon in the top right corner of chrome (this should bring up the options menu)
  2. 2) Set your options as you see fit
